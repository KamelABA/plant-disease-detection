Detectable Potato Diseases

Early Blight

Dark brown spots with concentric rings on leaves. Usually starts on older leaves.

Late Blight

Water-soaked lesions that turn brown or black. Can spread rapidly in humid conditions.

Healthy

Green, vibrant leaves with no visible spots or discoloration. Your plant is thriving!

Tips for Better Results

  • Take photos in natural daylight for best results
  • Ensure the leaf is clearly visible and in focus
  • Capture the affected areas if disease symptoms are visible
  • Avoid blurry or shaky images
